About Us

Our Central American culture teaches us family values of contribution, support, and family unity, love for our loved ones and friends. We were taught that it is better to give than to receive, and instilled with a seed and a sense of belonging to our countries. That is why CENTRO CARGO is born with the intention of continuing our values and family legacies of unity, and distance will not be a limit to get closer and support our families, organizations, and institutions that wish to send a small gift of love.

At CENTRO CARGO, we are your shipping center, safe, reliable, and friendly. We, as Central Americans, are in Washington and cross borders to reach your loved ones.

Mission

We are makers and pioneers who constantly challenge the limits of what is possible to reach our family and friends in Central America, providing excellent service to our customers. We are and will always be your reliable shipping center.

Vision

At Centro Cargo, we are and will continue to be committed to providing transportation and delivery services for messages and goods through companies and logistical channels focused on streamlining processes through technology, ensuring and shortening distances, always keeping the well-being of our Central American communities from 2023 to 2028 in mind.
